Chaska Valley Family Theatre

Chaska Valley Family Theatre hired me for their production of School House Rock: The Musical to create posters and illustrations for them as well as painting and designing props.

The posters were based off of the illustrations from the televisions show School House Rock and I made an illustration poster for each song in the musical. The posters were drawn using a Wacom tablet and Adobe Photoshop.
