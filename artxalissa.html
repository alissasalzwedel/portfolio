artxalissa.com

artxalissa is the brand I created for myself where I sell my art online. It was started in 2019 from my first dorm room at UWEC after I wondered what I could do with the little illustrations I liked making. After researching some of my favorite illustrators, I saw that many of them were turning their art into stickers, so I started doing that, too!
